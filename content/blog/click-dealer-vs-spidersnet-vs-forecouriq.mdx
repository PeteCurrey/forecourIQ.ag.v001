Choosing the right software platform to run your independent car dealership is one of the most consequential decisions you will make. Pick the wrong one, and you will be bogged down in administrative tasks, fighting with slow websites, and locked into expensive, inflexible contracts.

For years, the UK independent dealer market has been dominated by a handful of legacy providers. Click Dealer and Spidersnet are two of the most established names in the space. 

However, as we move into 2026, the technology landscape has shifted dramatically. AI integration, programmatic SEO, and cinematic website performance are no longer luxuries—they are baseline requirements. This has given rise to modern challengers like ForecourIQ.

Here is an objective, head-to-head comparison to help you decide which platform is right for your dealership.

## 1. Click Dealer: The Established Workhorse

Click Dealer is a giant in the independent sector. If you visit 100 independent forecourts, a significant percentage will be running their back office on Click.

**The Pros:**
- **Deep Feature Set:** Because they have been around for years, their DMS covers almost every obscure edge case a dealer might encounter.
- **Vast Integration Network:** They integrate with almost every third-party finance and lead provider in the UK.
- **Familiarity:** Many sales staff have used Click before, so onboarding new hires is often faster.

**The Cons:**
- **The "Add-On" Pricing Model:** Click's base package often looks appealing, but essential modern features (like advanced SEO modules, API access, or premium support) are frequently bolted on at an extra monthly cost. 
- **Website Aesthetics:** While functional, many of their website templates look dated and similar across thousands of dealerships. They can struggle with mobile performance scores compared to modern JavaScript frameworks.
- **Lack of Native AI Sourcing:** It remains primarily a reactive stock management tool rather than a proactive, AI-driven market intelligence engine.

## 2. Spidersnet: The Agency Approach

Spidersnet has historically positioned itself as a digital marketing agency that also provides dealer software, heavily emphasizing website design and lead generation.

**The Pros:**
- **Design Focus:** Their bespoke website builds often look visually superior to the standard templates offered by other legacy competitors.
- **Marketing Services:** They offer managed SEO, Google Ads, and social media management as an agency service alongside the software.
- **Reliable Feeds:** Their stock syndication to AutoTrader and Motors.co.uk is generally robust and reliable.

**The Cons:**
- **Fragmented Experience:** Often, the website and the back-office DMS feel like two separate products stitched together, rather than one unified platform.
- **Agency Costs:** If you want them to handle your SEO and marketing, the monthly retainer fees can escalate quickly, making it an expensive option for smaller independent dealers.
- **Speed Issues:** Like many older platforms, the websites can suffer from slow load times, especially when handling massive image galleries on mobile networks.

## The Verdict: Which Should You Choose?

The decision ultimately comes down to what you want your software to do.

**Choose Click Dealer if:** You value extreme familiarity, need integration with very specific legacy software, and don't mind paying extra add-on fees for an established name.

**Choose Spidersnet if:** You want a provider who will also act as your digital marketing agency (and you have the budget to pay the retainer fees for those services).

**Choose ForecourIQ if:** You want the fastest website in your local area, you are tired of opaque pricing models, and—crucially—you want AI data tools that actively help you buy the right stock to maximize your net profit. 

In 2026, relying purely on gut instinct to buy cars is a losing strategy. The dealers who adopt AI-driven market intelligence are the ones who will scale.
